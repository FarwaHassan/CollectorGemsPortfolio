About Us

Collector Gems Ltd. is a platform for high-class rough and cut crystals, rare minerals, and collectibles. We offer precious and semi-precious gemstones and specimen sourced from around the world. Ruby, Emerald, Sapphire, Garnet, Tourmaline and Peridot are just to name a few among those offered.

Hassan Majeed established the company with a clear vision of bringing gems and specimen to the world directly from the mines to the market. A passionate mountaineer and gemologist himself, he takes pride in showcasing his finds.

We strongly believe in quality, integrity and service. All our gemstones are real, untreated and kept so as found in nature. Rough stones can also be cut and polished into faceted gems as per clients requirement. Our team of certified gemologists, appraisers and gem cutters handle them with utmost care and professionalism.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a Crystal and a Gemstone?

Crystal is the raw natural formation of a mineral as found in nature, whereas, Gemstone is the cut and polished form of the mineral.

Are pearls the same as minerals?

Pearls are obtained from living organisms, therefore, not the same as rock minerals in their composition. Gemologists categorise them as Semi-Precious Organic Gems.

How do you tell if the gemstone is real?

The easiest way is the touch test. A natural stone is always cold to touch. Then look inside the gemstone through a 20x loop to see if there are any visible natural inclusions. Take measurements like Refractive Index, Birefringence and Specific Gravity of the gemstone using special equipment for each. Lastly, if still not clear, check the gemstone under a microscope to see any signs of naturally occuring formation lines and/or inclusions.
